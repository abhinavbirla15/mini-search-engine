The Rise of Streaming ServicesStreaming services revolutionized entertainment, shifting from cable TV to on-demand platforms like Netflix, Hulu, and Disney+. Launched in 2007, Netflix pioneered binge-watching with original content.Algorithms personalize recommendations, increasing engagement. Global expansion brings diverse content. Challenges include content piracy and market saturation.Economic impacts include job creation in production. As technology advances, interactive and VR experiences emerge. Streaming reshapes media consumption, offering convenience and variety.
