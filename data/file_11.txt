The Evolution of the InternetThe internet originated from ARPANET in 1969, a U.S. Department of Defense project for resilient communication. Tim Berners-Lee's invention of the World Wide Web in 1989 made it accessible, introducing hyperlinks and browsers.Growth exploded in the 1990s with dial-up connections and early search engines like Yahoo. Broadband and mobile devices transformed it into a global platform. Social media, e-commerce, and streaming services dominate today.Challenges include misinformation, privacy issues, and digital divides. Innovations like 5G and IoT promise faster, more connected experiences. The internet's evolution continues to shape society, economy, and culture worldwide.
