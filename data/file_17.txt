Ocean Conservation EffortsOceans cover 71% of Earth, hosting diverse ecosystems and regulating climate. Pollution, overfishing, and acidification threaten marine life. Plastic waste harms species like turtles and fish.Conservation initiatives include marine protected areas and sustainable fishing. Organizations like WWF advocate for policies. Coral reef restoration uses innovative techniques.Individual actions like reducing plastic use help. International agreements like the UN Ocean Decade promote research. Protecting oceans ensures biodiversity and human well-being for future generations.
