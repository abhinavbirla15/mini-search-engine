The Science of NutritionNutrition fuels the body with essential macronutrients (carbohydrates, proteins, fats) and micronutrients (vitamins, minerals). Balanced diets prevent diseases like obesity and diabetes. The Mediterranean diet emphasizes fruits, vegetables, and olive oil.Portion control and hydration are key. Myths like "fat-free" foods persist, but quality matters. Supplements aid deficiencies but aren't substitutes for whole foods.Cultural diets vary, from Japanese sushi to Indian spices. Research links nutrition to longevity. Educating on healthy eating promotes well-being.
