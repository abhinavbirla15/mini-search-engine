The History of PhotographyPhotography captures moments, invented by Joseph Nicéphore Niépce in 1826 with the first permanent image. Daguerreotypes popularized portraits in the 1840s. Film cameras dominated until digital revolution in the 2000s.Photographers like Ansel Adams mastered landscapes. Photojournalism documents history, from war to social movements. Smartphones democratize the art.Editing software like Photoshop enables creativity. Ethics include manipulation and consent. Photography preserves memories and inspires storytelling.
