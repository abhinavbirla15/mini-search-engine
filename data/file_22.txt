The Evolution of Music GenresMusic genres reflect cultural shifts, from classical symphonies to modern pop. Jazz emerged in the early 20th century, blending African rhythms with European harmony. Rock 'n' roll revolutionized youth culture in the 1950s.Hip-hop, born in the Bronx, addresses social issues through rap and beats. Electronic dance music (EDM) thrives in festivals. Streaming platforms like Spotify democratize access.Artists like Beethoven and Beyoncé influence generations. Fusion genres like reggaeton blend traditions. Music evolves with technology, fostering creativity and expression.
