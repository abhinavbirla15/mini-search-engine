Economic Principles and InflationEconomics studies resource allocation and wealth creation. Inflation measures rising prices, eroding purchasing power. Causes include demand-pull, cost-push, and monetary expansion.Central banks like the Federal Reserve use interest rates to control it. Hyperinflation devastated economies like Weimar Germany. Deflation, the opposite, can stifle growth.Policies like fiscal stimulus combat recessions. Understanding inflation aids personal finance and investment. Global trade and supply chains add complexity to economic stability.
