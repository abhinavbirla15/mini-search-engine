Urban Planning for SustainabilityUrban planning designs cities for livability and resilience. Sustainable practices include green spaces, public transit, and energy-efficient buildings. Smart cities use IoT for traffic and waste management.Challenges like sprawl and inequality require inclusive policies. Examples include Copenhagen's bike-friendly design and Singapore's vertical farming.Benefits include reduced emissions and improved health. Community involvement ensures equitable outcomes.
