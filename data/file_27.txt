Mental Health AwarenessMental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being. Conditions like anxiety and depression affect millions, often stigmatized. Therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes aid recovery.Workplace stress and social media contribute. Campaigns like World Mental Health Day raise awareness. Support networks and hotlines provide help.Prevention focuses on sleep, exercise, and mindfulness. Breaking stigma fosters empathy. Prioritizing mental health improves quality of life globally.
