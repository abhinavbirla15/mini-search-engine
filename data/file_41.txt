Mythology and Ancient LegendsMythology explains natural phenomena through stories of gods and heroes. Greek myths feature Zeus and Odysseus, exploring themes of fate and heroism. Norse legends involve Thor and Ragnarok.These tales influence literature and culture. Egyptian mythology centers on Ra and the afterlife. Modern interpretations appear in films like "Clash of the Titans."Myths preserve history and morals. Studying them reveals human psyche. They inspire art and philosophy across eras.
