The Impact of Social MediaSocial media connects billions via platforms like Facebook and TikTok. It amplifies voices, from activism to entertainment. Algorithms curate content, influencing opinions and behaviors.Benefits include networking and information sharing. Drawbacks encompass misinformation, cyberbullying, and addiction. Privacy concerns arise from data collection.Regulation like GDPR aims for transparency. Brands leverage it for marketing.
