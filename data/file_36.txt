The History of FashionFashion evolves with culture, from ancient Egyptian linen to Renaissance ruffles. The Industrial Revolution popularized ready-made clothing. Designers like Coco Chanel revolutionized women's wear.Trends reflect social changes, like 1960s mod styles. Sustainability drives eco-friendly materials. Fast fashion's environmental toll prompts ethical alternatives.Fashion expresses identity. Museums preserve iconic pieces. Innovation continues with wearable tech.
