Ancient Civilizations: The MayaThe Maya civilization flourished in Mesoamerica from 2000 BCE to 1500 CE, known for advanced astronomy, mathematics, and architecture. They developed a hieroglyphic writing system and a calendar predicting eclipses.Cities like Tikal and Chichen Itza featured pyramids and palaces. Agriculture sustained their population, with maize as a staple. Religion centered on gods and rituals, including human sacrifice.Decline came from environmental degradation and warfare. Artifacts like pottery and codices preserve their legacy. Modern Maya descendants continue traditions, offering insights into resilience and innovation.
